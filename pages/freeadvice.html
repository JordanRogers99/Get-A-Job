Free Advice

Our mission is to help enable you to find your feet with your first career in art. Whilst we also help more experienced artists to find work, this page is aimed at people that are looking to land their first job, or put a portfolio or creative CV (resume) together.

In order to assist you without charging a penny, this page is dedicated exclusively to helping you in these first steps.

Below, we are covering a few of the essentials for getting started, and then you can find all of the articles online that we found most useful to achieve what you are looking to do. We are trying to cover all our bases and make sure that we can offer some help to everybody, but if there is an area that we haven't included that you need help with then please do contact us and let us know what we're missing.

Do I need to create a special CV for the arts?

In short, we would say yes. It is crucial to the application process to show that you are looking for a job in the arts specifically. We have put together a guide, alongside a template, that will help you better understand what goes into this. Don't worry, we are assuming that you have no paid experience yet.

Can anyone make it in the art industry?

We like to believe that it is possible for anybody. In the case of our website, we are working on the English speaking world because the language of the founders is English. In time, we would like to expand worldwide. Our feeling is that it is not neccesarily the easiest industry to get into, we must be creative and approach this in an abstract way. There are no gauranteed entry points, but through trial and error, mixed with consistency, we do believe that it is possible for anyone.
